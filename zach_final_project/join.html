Become a member today

Non-Profit:

A Non-Profit membership is free for anyone. With a Non-Profit membership you will have access to any media posts made from the Rigby Chamber of Commerce and have a chance to win lunceon tickets every month.

Bronze:

A Bronze membership costs an annual fee of $200. With this membership you will have acces to any media posts made from the Rigby Chamber of Commerce, recieve luncheon tickets, and your business will be sponsered in the Jefferson Star newspaper.

Silver:

A Silver membership costs an annual fee of $400. With With this membership you will have acces to any media posts made from the Rigby Chamber of Commerce, recieve luncheon tickets, and your business will be sponsered in the Jefferson Star newspaper, and on our facebook page. Your business will also be added to our directory listings page.

Gold:

A Gold membership costs an annual fee of $1,000. With With this membership you will have acces to any media posts made from the Rigby Chamber of Commerce, recieve luncheon tickets, and your business will be sponsered in the Jefferson Star newspaper, and on our facebook page, and on our recommended business highlight page. Your business will also be added to our directory listings page, and we will provide business trainings.
