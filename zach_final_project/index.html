Welcome to the Rigby City Chamber of Commerce

The Rigby Chamber of Commerce is a local association that is committed to promote and protect the interests of business in the Rigby area. It is our mission to provide educational opportunities and assist businesses with the latest marketing and promotional techniques. We hope to insure additional customers and income to the business owners and people of the Rigby area.

Company Highlights

Broulims

The first Broulims' opened in Rigby in 1922. Sense then a new, bigger store has been built just off Main Street in Rigby. The owner Robert Broulim has continued to expand his business now reaching three states with 10 locations.

Pick Me Up

Are you thirsty? Well if you are then go to Pick Me Up. They serve a variety of sodas, hot chocolate, gatorade, powerade, and more. At Pick Me Up you can mix and match your favorite sodas, energy drinks, and flavored syrup to create the perfect drink for you. With so many different possible flavor combinations your satisfaciton is gaurunteed.

Wendys

Wendys is a great place to eat if you are looking for a cheap meal. With their 4 for 4 deal you can choose a drink, one side, and two main menu items for just 4 dollars! If that isn't a bargain then I don't know what its. They also serve more expensive but better quality food just like anywhere else.
