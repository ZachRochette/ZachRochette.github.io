Rigby

Rigby is located in Jefferson County Idaho. It is a small town with a current population of 4,438, sitting as the 36th largest city in Idaho. Set between two larger cities, Idaho Falls and Rexburg, Rigby has become a haven for those that like the small town feel but occasionally enjoy what the slightly larger towns have to offer. Many college students from Rexburg live in Rigby making the median age in Rigby 27 years old, 27.5 for males, and 26.3 for females. Although it might seem like life is a little slow here, Rigby is always hosting public events such as rodeos, farmers markets, and an annual car show. Between all that goes on, and the small town feel, Rigby isn't just a good place to settle down and retire but it is also a great place to grow up and raise a family.
