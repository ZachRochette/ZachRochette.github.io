The Best place to fish

Fish Haven is a beautiful town right next to Bear Lake. The lake stretches 50 miles and of course is the home of many fish. That is why this year we are celebrating "How Big Was That Fish Day". If you are a fisherman that loves fishing, or just simply like looking at and talking about fish then come visit us for How big was that fish day! It's family friendly and great fun for everyone.
